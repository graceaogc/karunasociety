Karuna Kendra Study Center

It is an unfortunate fact, that over 40% of the population in India lives on less than $2 per day. Majority of India's population survive below the poverty line. Many of us have grown tolerant to this statistic and have left the responsibility of helping them to the government or NGOs. And this might be simply because we believe that our efforts wouldn't solve the bigger problem anyway.

We believe that every life changed is a contribution towards reversing the statistics. Reaching lives one at a time. At our Karuna Kendra Study Centers we encourage children to attain basic education. We provide tutoring, supplies, tuition fee, clothing, and food. These study centers help them stay motivated to stay in school, better their lives and move out of their current situation.

We are fortunate to have wonderful teachers, counsellors and medical professionals who meet with the children and their parents/guardians on a regular basis to educate them in issues that are of importance and could change their habits bringing improvement in the society.
